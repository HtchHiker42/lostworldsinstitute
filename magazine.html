From the Editor

Welcome July 9, 2025

Welcome to LWI!

Welcome to our organization! Here's a quick intro.

The Lost Worlds Institute is a youth-led non-profit dedicated to preserving the world's most endangered languages. From Taa — with its extraordinary click consonant system — to Yuchi, a language isolate with no known relatives anywhere on Earth, we are documenting the voices that history has tried to silence.

LWI Magazine is where we tell those stories — in long-form features, cultural essays, research summaries, and community profiles. This is the place to learn about the communities behind the languages, the scholars doing the work, and the students driving the movement.

Siddharth Chopra
Founder & President · Roxbury Latin, Boston MA

Editorial Mission

What LWI Magazine Covers

Write articles and shorter posts about native groups, languages, and our achievements & events. Anyone who is writing-inclined should join us here and become part of our staff!

Long-Form Features

In-depth profiles of endangered language communities, their history, culture, and the forces threatening their survival. Our most ambitious journalism — deep research and original reporting on the languages and people we study.

Research Summaries

Accessible write-ups of our documentation findings — bringing the insights from our grammar sketches and phonetic analyses to a general audience who cares about languages but doesn't read academic papers.

Community Profiles

Portraits of the people behind the languages — native speakers, community elders, grassroots revitalization workers, and the scholars who have devoted their careers to preserving these communities.

Event Coverage & Updates

Write-ups of our Students × Scholar Forums, conference proceedings, and LWI news — so that the knowledge shared in each session reaches beyond the attendees and into the public record.
